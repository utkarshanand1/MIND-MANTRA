Emergency Calm Sheet

Use this when stress spikes suddenly.

1) Three Breathing Techniques
- Physiological sigh: inhale through nose, short second inhale, long exhale through mouth. Repeat 3 times.
- Box breathing: inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s. Repeat 4 cycles.
- 4-7-8: inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s. Repeat 3 cycles.

2) Three Grounding Prompts
- Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can feel, 3 you can hear.
- Press both feet into the floor and describe the sensation.
- Place one hand on your chest and one on your abdomen; track 5 breaths.

3) One Positive Reframing Exercise
- Current thought: "I cannot handle this."
- Reframe: "This is hard, but I can handle the next step."
- Next step (under 10 minutes): __________________
